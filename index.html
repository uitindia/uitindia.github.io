UiT The Arctic Univeristy of Norway

Scholarship-supported Master Program 2021

Application invited for a special scholarship-supported master program 2021 at the Faculty of Science and Technology, UiT The Arctic University of Norway.

This document contains the information about submitting an application for a special scholarship-supported master program 2021 at the Faculty of science and technology, UiT The Arctic University of Norway. In 2020, UiT had signed MoUs with the following Indian institutes of education:

  • Indian Institute of Technology, New Delhi, India.
  • Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad, India.
  • Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati, India.
  • Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology, New Delhi, India.

UiT The Arctic University of Norway is a medium-sized research university that contributes to knowledge-based development at the regional, national and international level.

UiT The Arctic University of Norway is the third largest in Norway and the northernmost university of the world. Its location on the edge of the Arctic implies a mission. The Arctic is of increasing global importance. Climate change, the exploitation of Arctic resources and environmental threats are topics of great public concern, and which the University of Tromsø takes special interest in. At UiT The Arctic University of Norway you can explore global issues from a close-up perspective.

15500 students and 3300 staff study and work at UiT The Arctic University of Norway. Teaching is research-based. UiT´s eight faculties offer, in spite of a dedication to Northern issues, a broad range of study programmes. The academic community in Tromsø is highly international. More than 20% of the academic staff and 10% of the student body are from abroad. The University offers more than 20 English taught master´s degree programmes, and all faculties offer English taught courses at both bachelor’s and master’s level.

As a follow up of MoU signed between UiT and four Indian Universities (IIT Delhi, IIT Guwahati, IIT(ISM) Dhanbad, and IIIT Delhi), and in order to support the joint research and education interests, the Faculty of Science and Technology at UiT has kindly organised a pilot master program for bachelor students who are either graduating in 2021 or have already graduated from the above institutions. Students from other institutions are not eligible for this special program, and may apply through the normal annual master program intake of UiT.

There are a maximum of five positions in this special program. The total amount of the scholarship is capped at 300,000 Norwegian Kroner(NOK) per student admitted under this scholarship program. Each student will be provided with monthly scholoarship stipend (amounting to NOK 123519 per year). Visa application fee and travel (economy fare) will be reimbursed after joining UiT. The students will not be levied tution fees. No declaration of financial ability is needed for the application and visa for students enrolled in this program.

Eligibility Criteria

Institute of Bachelor Degree

This is a special master degree with scholarship program. Only bachelor students (in the final year or already graduated) from the following four(4) Indian universities are eligible**.

  • Indian Institute of Technology, New Delhi, India.
  • Indian Institute of Technology (Indian School of Mines), Dhanbad, India.
  • Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati, India.
  • Indraprastha Institute of Information technology, New Delhi, India.

** UiT The Arctic University of Norway has signed MoUs (memorandum of understanding) with the above four Indian universities.
Degree Requirement (any one)
  • All India Senior School Certificate (Standard 12) issued by CBSE + 4 year university education.
  • Indian School Certificate (ISC Standard 12) issued by CISCE + 4 year university education.
  • Higher Secondary Certificate issued by State Secondary School Boards + 4 year university education.
  • Pre-University Examination Certificate + 4 year university education.
  • Intermediate Examination Certificate + 4 year university education.
Proof of English Proficiency (any one)
  • TOEFL - Official score report: Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) with a minimum score of 580 (paper-based)/ 213 (computer-based)/ 85 (Internet-based) or higher. Send the original TOEFL Report to the Institutional code: 0742.
  • IELTS -Academic test: International English Language Testing Service (IELTS) with a minimum of 6.5 points or higher.
  • PTE Academic: Pearson Test of English Academic with minimum score of 62.
  • APIEL: Advanced Placement International English Language Examination with a minimum of 3 points.
  • MELAB: Michigan English Language Assessment Battery with a minimum of 85 points.
  • University of Cambridge Exams: Certificate in Advanced English, Certificate of Proficiency in English.
Minimum Bachelor Level Grade Criteria
  • CGPA of 7.5 out of 10.0
  • 3,00 of 4, 00 (75%) or higher.
  • Note: The Admission Office at UiT verifies submitted bachelor's degree diploma/transcript with the issuing school/institution. If we are unable to verify your qualifications, you will not be offered admission..

Selection process:

Due to the limited number of positions and scholarship support, the selection is competitive and is based on a combination of several factors. Based on the submitted application, an initial shortlist will be performed to identify the candidates for the next stage and contacted.

Candidates shortlisted will appear for interviews. For master in computer science* applications, a code task must be submitted with the application package (https://en.uit.no/Content/596000/Programming_task.pdf), other master applicants may also be given some test tasks after the shortlisting.

Candidates will be selected based on CV, cover letter, overall performance in interview and test task(s), personal suitability of the candidate, and other factors.
